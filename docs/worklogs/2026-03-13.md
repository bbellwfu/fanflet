### Bugs found and fixed

1. **`getClientIp` empty-string bug** (`lib/visitor-hash.ts`): `??` operator didn't catch empty `x-forwarded-for` strings. Changed to `||`. Would have caused blank IPs in analytics for visitors behind certain proxies.

2. **`classifyReferrer` regex ordering** (`lib/referrer.ts`): `mail.google.com` was classified as "search" instead of "email" because the `google.` search regex matched before the `mail.` email regex. Reordered checks. Would have inflated search referral numbers and zeroed out Gmail email referrals.
